CyberPatriot

Man page

CyberPatriot is the National Youth Cyber Education Program created by the Air Force Association. During the competition You are assigned virtual machines to find vulnerabilities and patch them in order to gain points. Competitors are also tested on networking concepts. During the National Finals competition competitors are given 7-8 Windows and Linux servers with critical services to defend against professional white hat hackers (red team).

Capture The Flag [CTFs]

Man page

CTFs are a offensive (can also be defensive) cybersecurity competition. Competitors are tasked with completing a large variety of challenges to get a "flag" (solution). CTF challenges include Web exploitation, Reverse Engineering, Binary Exploitation, Digital Forensics, Open Source Intelligence, and much more! Many of these competitions are held online with competitors from all around the world.
